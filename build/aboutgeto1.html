His Cursed Technique,
Cursed Spirit Manipulation, allows him to absorb and control
natural curses, which turn into black orbs that Geto must orally
ingest to command the cursed spirits. Suguru Geto's descent
into villainy in Jujutsu Kaisen stems from a disillusionment
with his role as a jujutsu sorcerer and a deep-seated resentment
towards non-sorcerers.
